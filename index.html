Outdoor collective

We envision a community with equitable access to nature and a shared commitment to conserve it.

We are a network of local educators, outdoor enthusiasts and youth allies who reduce barriers and expand opportunities to get youth outdoors in Monterey County. We envision a community with equitable access to nature and a shared commitment to conserve it.

Our goals

We work by supporting collaboration among youth-serving organizations and park and open space groups to create shared outdoor education resources.

  • Encourage support and collaboration among outdoor education and youth-serving organizations by gathering regularly.
  • Set up a lending library for outdoor gear, and training for youth leaders to borrow camping equipment and other outdoor gear.
